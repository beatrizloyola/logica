This pack contains the OBJ and FBX files so you can decide what format is best for whatever project

you are working on.



Just a heads up, if you desire that heavily pixelated look like most PSX styles or in my screenshots,

you will most likely have to edit the interpolation on the textures on whatever engine you're importing

to. For Blender, this is changing "linear" to "closest. For Unity and Unreal, this is most likely

"point" and "nearest" respectively.
